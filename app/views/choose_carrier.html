Is your company the carrier for this consignment?

Help with carriers

The carrier is defined as the operator of the 'active means of transport'.

This means the vessel, aircraft, train or road vehicle used to bring the goods into Great Britain.

For example, if a freight truck is on a ferry and unaccompanied, the ferry company is the carrier.

If a truck is accompanied and driven on and off the ferry by its driver, the haualge company is the carrier.
